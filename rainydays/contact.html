shipping & delivery

RAINYDAYS offers Ground and Express shipping options depending on the order type and destination. The estimated delivery time for Ground shipments is 3-4 business days, while Express shipments can take 2-3 business days. We work hard to get your order to you as fast as possible, however occasionally there may be an unexpected delay outside of our control which may impact the estimated delivery time. RAINYDAYS online purchases can be shipped to destinations within Canada, USA, Germany, UK, Ireland, Sweden, Denmark, Norway, France, Austria, Poland, Switzerland, Spain, Japan, Italy, Belgium, Czech Republic, Finland, Australia and the Netherlands. Arc'teryx does not allow shipping to addresses offering freight forwarding services. After your order has been processed and is ready to leave our distribution center, you will receive a shipping confirmation email with the tracking number. To check the status, you can Track Your Order.
